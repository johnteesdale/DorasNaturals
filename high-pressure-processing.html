WHAT IS HIGH PRESSURE PROCESSING (HPP)?

HPP is a food safety and preservation method that uses only cold water and extreme pressure to safely and naturally preserve juices and beverages. This alleviates any need for preservatives and/or harsh chemicals to achieve fresh longer lasting beverages.

USES UP TO 87,000 PSI

FOOD SAFETY

Destroys harmful bacteria and pathogens, without getting rid of beneficial vitamins and minerals

LONGER SHELF-LIFE

This high pressure process greatly extends the product shelf-life

Eco-Friendly
